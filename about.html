What is bb-stack.net?

bb-stack.net is a little side-project by me. One day I realized that I had a lot of devices laying around that all provided a lot of computational power. In a little project I built my own custom private cloud computing service from where I operated VMs as I wished, which became insanely useful during my PhD. bb-stack.net is an attempt to make my computing resources also available to others. Mainly, research and open-source projects are in focus.

Priorization

  1. Research & Open-Source Projects in Berlin-Brandenburg Region.
  2. Research & Open-Source Projects in Germany.
  3. Other Research & Open-Source Project.
    However, requests might be denied due to missing proof of qualification.

What about costs?

The service is free of charge. However, resources are highly limited and may not be approved. If you have the ability to add computational power, feel free to get in contact.

Can I support this project?

Sure! Either contribute computational resources by hosting a compute node or do a donation: Buy me a Coffee.
